About BOTSOGO

Promoting health and wellness in Botswana, one step at a time.

Our Vision

BOTSOGO was created as part of a school project by a Computer Systems Engineering student, with the goal of spreading awareness about healthy living through reliable information tailored to the needs of Botswana’s communities.

Why We Exist

Many people in Botswana face growing challenges related to poor nutrition, lack of exercise, and mental health awareness. BOTSOGO is designed to bring together local stories, medical knowledge, and helpful resources to educate and empower people.

What You Can Expect

  • Guides on healthy eating using local ingredients
  • Exercise routines suited for home or community use
  • Insights on mental wellness with Botswana-specific context
  • Tips, videos, and testimonies that are easy to relate to

Our Motto

“Small steps lead to strong health.”
